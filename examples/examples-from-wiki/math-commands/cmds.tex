First of all, in paragraphs. Math commands will be replaced in inline math only: $a \geq b$ works,
but this <= will not be replaced.

\begin{equation}
  a^2 + b^2 \geq c^2
\end{equation}
\begin{equation}
  a^2 + b^2 <= c^2
\end{equation}

And in prefs:
\begin{align}
  a^2 + b^2 \geq c^2
\end{align}
\begin{align}
  a^2 + b^2 <= c^2
\end{align}
